Favourite Anime/Manga:

"One Piece is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Eiichiro Oda.he story follows the adventures of Monkey D. Luffy, a boy whose body gained the properties of rubber after unintentionally eating a Devil Fruit. With his pirate crew, the Straw Hat Pirates, Luffy explores the Grand Line in search of the deceased King of the Pirates Gol D. Roger's ultimate treasure known as the "One Piece" in order to become the next King of the Pirates."

After I started reading One Piece last year, it quickly became one of my favourites. Although it is a very long series, it has a very interesting storyline!

"Katekyo Hitman Reborn! is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Akira Amano. It follows Tsunayoshi Sawada, a young boy who discovers that he is next in line to become boss of the Vongola family, a powerful Mafia organization. The Vongolas' most powerful hitman, a gun-toting infant named Reborn, is sent to teach Tsuna how to be a boss."

Hitman Reborn was one of the first anime I came across when I was younger and I believe that it will always have a special place in my heart! It's very underrated in my opinion.
